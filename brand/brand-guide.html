06 — Voice & Tone

✓ Do say
  • "Find your fourth" — insider language
  • "Tap in" — short, action-first
  • "5 players here now" — real-time, specific
  • "Courts are live" — energy, urgency
  • "Your crew is at Hollenbeck" — personal, social
✗ Don't say
  • "Connect with players" — generic, LinkedIn energy
  • "Optimize your game" — fitness app cliché
  • "Building community" — vague marketing speak
  • "Premium experience" — not what this is about
  • "Track your performance" — wrong app, wrong vibe
